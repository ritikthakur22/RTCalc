## 3. Long Description (Max 4000 characters)
Welcome to **RTCalc**—the ultimate modern calculator designed for simplicity, speed, and premium usability. Styled with an elegant, iPhone-inspired interface and optimized for both Android phones and tablets, RTCalc makes everyday calculations effortless.

Whether you're performing basic arithmetic, managing personal finances, or calculating complex percentages, RTCalc provides high-precision results instantly.

### 🌟 Key Features:
- **High-Precision Engine**: Built using robust decimal math libraries to avoid standard floating-point calculation errors (e.g. 0.1 + 0.2 is exactly 0.3).
- **Elegant Expression History**: Never lose track of your work. Save, copy, or restore previous calculations with the built-in persistent history.
- **Beautiful Adaptive Themes**: Toggle between a clean White Light Mode, a pure Pitch-Black Dark Mode, or match your device's system settings.
- **Premium Haptic Feedback**: Feel every key press with physical haptic feedback that simulates a premium mechanical button (can be toggled on/off in settings).
- **Responsive / Adaptive Layouts**: Fits perfectly on all screens. Enjoy a persistent side-by-side history panel on tablets and landscape screens, or an elegant slide-up sheet on phones.
- **Large Scaling Display**: Numbers auto-scale dynamically so long equations remain easy to read without breaking onto new lines.
- **One-Tap Actions**: Copy results instantly to the clipboard with a simple long-press on the display.
- **Standard Calculator Operations**: Addition (+), Subtraction (-), Multiplication (×), Division (÷), percentage calculations (%), decimal entry, positive/negative toggles (±), backspace deletion (⌫), and Clear All (AC).

### 🔒 Built with Modern Tech:
RTCalc is engineered using industry best practices including Jetpack Compose for fluid UI animations, Room database for secure local history storage, Hilt for dependency injection, and DataStore for persistent settings. 

Get the clean, minimal, and powerful calculator you deserve. **Download RTCalc today!**

## 8. Privacy Policy Text
**RTCalc Privacy Policy**

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

**1. Information Collection and Use**
RTCalc is designed to respect your privacy. The application performs all computations locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, location data, usage statistics, or calculation history. 

**2. Data Security & Storage**
Your calculation history is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database (Room). This database is private to the application and is never uploaded to any remote server. You can clear this history at any time by tapping the "Clear History" button in the app.

**3. Permissions**
RTCalc requires no special permissions (e.g. no internet access, no contacts access, no location tracking) to operate.

**4. Changes to This Privacy Policy**
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this policy periodically for any changes.
